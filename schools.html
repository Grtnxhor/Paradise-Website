Paradise... Every Child Is Unique

  • Nusery

The Nusery School comprises of children between ages 2 and 5. The school emphasis montesorri method of teaching at this category. Our Nursery classes include;

  • Preparatory Diamond,
  • Nusery 1 Gold,
  • Nusery 2 Bronze and
    • Nusery 3 Silver.

The Basic classes is made up of pupils from ages 5+ to 10+. The Pupil in this category are exposed to basic education for six(6) years
leading to the award of First School Leaving Certificate

The College arm of the school is grouped into two:

  • Jumior School and
  • Senior School

Junior School comprises of J.S.S 1 - 3 and are presented at the end of the three(3) years for B.E.C.E Examination leading to the award of Basic Education Certificate.
The Senior School comprises of S.S.S 1-3. The Final class in this category write various certificate examination such as WAEC, NECO and GCE.

PARADISE SCHOOL formerly Future Hope Nusery and Primary School started September 18, 2000. The name was later changed to PARADISE MODEL SCHOOL on October 8, 2001, when the school was offically registered with the Ogun State Ministry of Education, Science and Technology.
