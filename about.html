Brief History About the School

PARADISE SCHOOL started September 18, 2000 bearing the name Future Hope Nusery and Primary School. The name was later changed to PARADISE MODEL SCHOOL on October 8, 2001, when the school was offically registered with the Ogun State Ministry of Education, Science and Technology

The school got her approval from the Ogun state ministry of Education on 4th June, 2010 under the name PARADISE NUSERY AND PRIMARY SCHOOL. The Cooperate Affairs Commission (C.A.C) regonized the school by granting the school certificate of registration as a business under the name PARADISE MODEL SCHOOL on February 18, 2010.
